Paulo Murillo

Ph.D Student·Oregon State University· murillop@oregonstate.edu

Satellite and aerial remote sensing allow us to monitor many different phenomena on Earth surface. I am leveraged of the huge amount of satellite imagery for change detection analysis on landscape and its connection with socio-environmental issues such as armed conflict.
